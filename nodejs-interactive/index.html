Node.js Interactive Europe 2016 x NodeSchool

September 16th at de Beurs van Berlage

We're excited to bring NodeSchool to Node.js Interactive Europe!


NodeSchool is a community-driven effort with hundreds of local chapters throughout the world that aim to teach essential programming skills. NodeSchool organizes in-person workshops and builds on an open source curriculum that teaches various Node.js and JavaScript skills in an interactive, self-guided format.

Join your fellow attendees in one of the sessions and have the friendly organizers of NodeSchool Amsterdam and other members of the NodeSchool and Node.js communities help you through the sessions.
